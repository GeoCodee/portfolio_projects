Instructions

The goal of this game is to defeat the boss Kefka. You will have four characters. Swordsman, Fighter, Mage, and Healer. Each character will have their own set of unique skills and attributes.

    Each character will have 4 options.
  • Attack
  • Defend
  • Ultimate
  • Items

    • *Pro tip: it's not recommended to have all characters on defense.
